Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9 - inch pie

30 Min - Prep Time

40 Min-total

8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all - purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tab salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juice and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 eggs yolks
  • 4 eggs whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  1. Prehart Oven : Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degree C)
  2. Make Lemon Filling : In a medium sauce pan
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar , flour , conrn starch and salt
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • stir in butter
    • remove from heat
    • Pour filling
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy
    • Add sugar gradully
    • Spread Meringue over pie
  4. Bake : Baked in preheated oven for 10 mins
